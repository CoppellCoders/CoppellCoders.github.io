About Us

All meetings are held at Coppell High School and take place at 4:10 - 4:40 every Tuesday in room E102.

The App Development Club offers young learners from 9th - 12th grade the opportunity to advance skills in:

  • Planning
  • Collaboration
  • Management
  • Software programming
  • Interface development
